Department of Information Systems

Welcome to the Department of Information Systems at the University of Maryland, Baltimore County (UMBC). Our department is dedicated to providing students with the skills and knowledge they need to excel in the rapidly growing field of information systems. As a student in our programs, you'll have access to our renowned faculty members, who are experts in their fields and passionate about helping students achieve their academic and professional goals. Our curriculum combines theory with hands-on experience, allowing you to develop practical skills that are highly valued by employers. We invite you to explore our website and learn more about our programs, research, and community. At UMBC's Department of Information Systems, we are committed to preparing you for success in the exciting and ever-changing world of technology.
